# GrimfallTalentOverview

A lightweight Grimfall-WoW addon that consolidates all your active talent bonuses into a single view.

## How to Use

1. Open your Talents (default key: **N**).
2. Click the **[Summary]** button at the bottom right of the frame.
3. The overview will appear on the right side of the UI.

## Installation

1. Download the repository.
2. Place the `GrimfallTalentOverview` folder into your `\Grimfall\Games\Interface\AddOns` directory.
